The Perception Gap

Most individuals categorize all stress as harmful ("Distress"). However, psychological research indicates that viewing stress as a neutral energy source ("Eustress") significantly alters our physical and mental outcomes.

The Yerkes-Dodson Law

Performance doesn't peak when we are entirely relaxed. It peaks under moderate physiological arousal. Too little stress leads to apathy and boreout; too much leads to anxiety and burnout. Harnessing stress means surfing the peak of this bell curve.

Threat vs. Challenge Response

When you appraise a stressor as a threat, blood vessels constrict. When appraised as a challenge, cardiac output increases, vessels dilate, and the brain receives maximum oxygen. It is the same adrenaline, mapped to a different physical outcome.
